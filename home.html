My Hobby: Doodling

What is Doodling?

A doodle is a rough drawing made absentmindedly, according to Oxford Languages. Even famous people like to doodle from time to time, such as Queen Victoria and Mark Twain. There's a French illustrator named Roland Topor whose doodle became famous from its absurdity.

Why Do I Like Doodling?

Doodling helps me relax and get ready for my next project. I dont have to think about anything too strenuous and I can just let myself go in a doodle. I don't have to apologize for a doodle. I don't have to explain why I did something. I can just do it. Doodling can also be fun at times. I wouldn't normally think of it as "fun," but if you collaborate with someone or let someone draw a shape that you have to create into something, it can be challenging and fun.
